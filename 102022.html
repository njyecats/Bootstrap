Reading for October 3, 2022



   

The Magician


   
For today's card, I was shuffling, and The Magician fell out of the deck. That is one way of knowing it is the right card. Sometimes one card may stick out or "grab" your finger. Sometimes, it's all about using your intuition

The Magician is about new beginnings. This is the first card in the deck. If you look at the card, the magician is holding a wand or candle burning at both ends. One end points to the sky, the other end points to the earth. In addition, the magician has a pentacle, cup, sword, and a wand on the table in front of him. Also, there are flowers blooming all around the card. Lastly, the magician has an infinity symbol above his head.

This is all about beginnings, new, hope, and infinite possibilities. The cup, sword, pentacle, and wand represent water, air, earth, and fire. The Magician is a very positive card with lots of opportunities.

The Magician is about opportunities for the future. These opportunities can be all about love, careers, or even financial opportunities. Pursuing using your skills to make money.

The Magician is a wonderful choice for where I am in my life right now. I want a new career and this card give me hope that someday soon I will be able to start looking for that new career opportunity. I am ready for that new chapter in my life to begin.
